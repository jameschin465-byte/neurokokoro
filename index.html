The 5 Brain Skills We Train

Every NeuroKokoro program is built around these five core cognitive pillars — the mental skills that shape how your child thinks, learns, and responds every single day.

🧠
Attention

Helps your child stay focused, listen fully, and complete what they start — the foundation of all learning.

📅
Working Memory

Remembering 3 instructions at once without needing them repeated. The brain skill behind following through.

🤔
Decision-Making

Pausing to think before acting. Making better choices under pressure — at school and at home.

📐
Spatial Awareness

Understanding direction, distance and patterns — from packing a school bag to reading, writing and maths.

🔄
Cognitive Flexibility

Adapting calmly when plans change. The most important skill for a fast-changing world.
